Theme: ‘Road map for inclusive growth of the nation’

Shanghai, the city which is paving way to China’s glorious future bore witness to Educon 2012. Sixty educationists from India attended the event to discuss 'Innovative Education Practices in Universities – The road map for inclusive growth of the nation’ which was the year’s theme for Educon.

The most significant section of the Educon event was the visit to the Shanghai Technology Innovation Center (STIC). A non-profit public Institution under the direct leadership of S&T Commission of Shanghai Municipality, STIC was established in April, 1988 and is devoted to transferring technology achievements, incubating technology enterprises, and cultivating technology entrepreneurs. The Centre strives to build an excellent innovation environment to provide comprehensive services to technology enterprises and innovators for the commercialization, industrialization and internationalization of hi-tech technology achievements. The delegates were able to comprehend superior methods and approaches of the teaching and learning experience and were able to explore the various ways our education system can be transformed.

Theme: ‘Transformation for Excellence through Best Practices in building a University’

Istanbul (Turkey), the city of seven hills, is a blissful blend of cultures from the west and the east, and to our great joy served as the home for Sakal Educon, 2013. Sixty educationists from all over India were present in the event and discussed the year’s theme, ‘Transformation for Excellence through Best Practices in building a University’.

During the event, the delegates had an opportunity to visit the ‘Istanbul Technical University’. The theme was bifurcated into the following 7 sub topics: Academic Leadership, Financial Models for Universities, Research and Innovation, Academics, Technology and Education, Infrastructure, and Bridging Academia-Industry gap. Many intellectual figures including Principals of prestigious Institutions like IIT and Vice Chancellors of leading Central as well as State Universities graced the occasion with their presence.

Theme: ‘Quality in private-sector higher education in India, Skills and personality development in higher education, Internationalization element of higher education, Transparency and accountability in management of higher education’

Celebrating a decade of making education a richer experience, Sakal Educon 2014 was organized in the city of lights, Paris (France), and attended by a delegation of sixty educationists from across India. The delegates came together to contribute their opinions on the year’s theme, ‘The Higher Education System’. Several reflections on sub-topics such as ‘Quality of higher education’, ‘Skill & Personality Development’, ‘International Element and Transparency & Accountability Management’ were made.

Many prestigious and influential individuals including Vice Chancellors of leading Central and State Universities as well as premiers of prestigious Institutions like IIT, AICTE, MSBTE, etc. graced the occasion. The delegates also visited University of Paris Sorbonne 2014 & The Paris Institute of Political Studies, simply referred to as Sciences Po which is a public research and higher education institution in Paris, France.

Agenda:
a) Speaker sessions on :
 Analysis of higher education – Indian status, International status, Qualities of world-class universities, Academic programs, Quality of human resource, Quality of infrastructure, Pedagogy innovations, Curriculum design, Governance and administration, Financial administration, Culture and sports in academic institutions, Skill development.
b) Working sessions on:
 Faculty, curriculum and pedagogy, Finance & Automation, Governance and Administration in Private and Public Universities.
